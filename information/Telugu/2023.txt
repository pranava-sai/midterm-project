In the year 1985, Deva and Vardharaja "Vardha" Mannar, close companions in the city-state of Khansaar, face a tumultuous past involving tribal conflicts, betrayals, and revenge. Deva vows to return to Khansaar on Vardha's call. In 2017, a complex web of conspiracies unfolds as Deva, now entangled in the political strife of Khansaar, endeavors to protect Aadhya, the daughter of a business tycoon. The story delves into the intricate history of Khansaar, with themes of loyalty, vengeance, and hidden identities shaping the narrative.

Actors: Prabhas, Shruti Haasan
Director: Prashanth Neel
