A police officer named Shankar falsely accused of corruption takes refuge in the home of a kind-hearted woman, disguising himself as Ajay, her long-lost cousin. While investigating his father's death, he discovers a web of corruption within the police force and confronts his own past. As Shankar unravels the truth, he seeks justice against those who framed him.

Actors: Mahesh Babu, Samantha
Director: Srinu Vaitla
