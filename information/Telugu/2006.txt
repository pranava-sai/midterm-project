A fearless and crafty undercover cop, Pandu, infiltrates the criminal underworld to dismantle a powerful mafia network led by Ali Bhai. While navigating a perilous world of deception, Pandu discovers unexpected alliances and faces personal challenges. Starring Mahesh Babu and Ileana D'Cruz, directed by Puri Jagannadh.

Actors: Mahesh Babu, Ileana D'Cruz
Director: Puri Jagannadh
