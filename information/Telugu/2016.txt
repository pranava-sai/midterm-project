A mechanic named Anand, played by Jr. NTR, joins a garage known for its commitment to environmental causes and social welfare. An unexpected revelation connects Anand to the owner of the garage, Sathyam, portrayed by Mohanlal, who is revered for his dedication to the community. As Anand delves deeper into the garage's activities, he becomes embroiled in a conflict with a powerful businessman's son-in-law, played by Unni Mukundan. The film explores themes of environmental consciousness, social responsibility, and the consequences of unchecked power.

Actors: Jr. NTR, Mohanlal, Unni Mukundan, Samantha
Director: Koratala Siva
