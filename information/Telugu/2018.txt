A young man named Veera Raghava Reddy returns to his village to put an end to the longstanding feud between two rival factions, led by Narappa Reddy and Basi Reddy, following the death of his father. Amidst the chaos of violence and vengeance, he meets Aravinda, a woman determined to bring peace to their troubled community. Together, they strive to break the cycle of bloodshed and forge a path towards reconciliation. 

Actors: Jr. NTR, Pooja Hegde
Director: Trivikram Srinivas
