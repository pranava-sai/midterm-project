In the movie "RRR," set in the pre-independence era of India, two revolutionaries, Alluri Sitarama Raju and Komaram Bheem, unite against the British Raj and Nizam of Hyderabad. Their journey is marked by valor, sacrifice, and the pursuit of freedom. As they navigate through challenges and conflicts, their bond strengthens, propelling them towards their shared goal of liberating their people from oppression.

Actors: N. T. Rama Rao Jr., Ram Charan
Director: S. S. Rajamouli
