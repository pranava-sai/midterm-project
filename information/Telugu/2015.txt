A tribal warrior named Shivudu, unaware of his royal lineage, discovers the ancient kingdom of Mahishmati and falls in love with Avantika. Learning about his destiny and the betrayal surrounding his mother, Shivudu transforms into the powerful warrior Baahubali. The film delves into political conspiracies, family dynamics, and epic battles as Baahubali seeks justice and uncovers the truth about his heritage.

Actors: Prabhas, Anushka Shetty, Tamanna Bhatia
Director: S. S. Rajamouli
