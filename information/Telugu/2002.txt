In a Rayalaseema village, two feuding clans perpetuate constant violence, and a marriage proposal aimed at reconciliation backfires, leading to tragedy. Indrasena 'Indra' Reddy emerges as the new clan leader in the aftermath. The narrative then shifts to 2002 Varanasi, where Shankar Narayana's honest life becomes entangled in a complex love story. The revelation of his true identity as Indrasena Reddy adds further complexity. Upon Indra's return to his village, family secrets are exposed, setting off a chain of events. The film unfolds with a powerful message emphasizing unity and resolution in the face of longstanding conflict.

Actors: Chiranjeevi, Sonali Bindre, Aarti Agarwal
Director: B.Gopal
