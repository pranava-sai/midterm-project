A young man, Bantu, discovers his true lineage after being swapped at birth due to a hospital error. Raised in a middle-class family, he confronts the stark contrast with his wealthy biological family, led by the stern Valmiki. Bantu endeavors to bridge the emotional gap with his newfound family while navigating love, corporate dynamics, and uncovering Valmiki's ulterior motives. As he strives for acceptance and justice, Bantu's journey becomes a poignant exploration of familial bonds. 

Actors: Allu Arjun, Pooja Hegde
Director: Trivikram Srinivas
