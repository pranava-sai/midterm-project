A brave warrior from the past, Kala Bhairava, reincarnates in the present as Harsha, a bike stuntman, to fulfill his unfinished mission of avenging his lover's death and protecting his kingdom's honor. Entwined in a tale of love, betrayal, and reincarnation, Harsha discovers his past life and battles against evil forces to restore justice. 

Actors: Ram Charan, Kajal Aggarwal
Director: S.S. Rajamouli
