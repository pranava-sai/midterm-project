Raja, a small-time crook, unexpectedly finds himself in the afterlife and faces the consequences of his misdeeds. When he outwits Lord Yama, the god of death, Raja is granted a second chance at life. Upon returning, he realizes the power of good deeds and embarks on a journey to reform his ways. Starring Jr. NTR and Priyamani, directed by S. S. Rajamouli.

Actors: Jr. NTR, Priyamani
Director: S. S. Rajamouli
