Two brothers, Ram and Lakshman, lead contrasting lives; Ram is a strict, law-abiding police officer, while Lakshman embraces a carefree, adventurous lifestyle. Their worlds collide when Ram is assigned to apprehend the notorious crime lord, Shiva Reddy. As the brothers join forces, their unique approaches create both chaos and comic situations.

Actors: Allu Arjun, Shaam, Shruti Haasan
Director: Surender Reddy
