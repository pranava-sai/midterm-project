Shankar Prasad, a crime lord in Hyderabad, conceals his true identity when his father wishes him to be a doctor. His elaborate plan unravels when a marriage proposal exposes his criminal life. Driven by grief and a desire for redemption, he decides to pursue a medical degree. Despite minimal medical skills, Shankar transforms those around him with compassion and unconventional methods at Osmania College of Medical Sciences. His departure, prompted by guilt, leads to a miraculous awakening. Shankar marries Sunitha, realizing her true identity, and becomes Shankar Dada MBBS as his parents reconcile.

Actor: Chiranjeevi
Director: Jayanth C. Paranjee
