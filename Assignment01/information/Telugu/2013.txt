A wealthy businessman's grandson, Gautham Nanda, embarks on a journey to reconcile with his estranged aunt, Sunanda, as per his grandfather's dying wish. Disguised as Siddharth, a driver, he infiltrates Sunanda's household and forms deep connections with her family members. Along the way, he uncovers hidden truths, confronts past grievances, and strives to unite his fractured family against all odds.

Actors: Pawan Kalyan, Samantha Ruth Prabhu
Director: Trivikram Srinivas
