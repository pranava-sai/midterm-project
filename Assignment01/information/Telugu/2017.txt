After learning about his true lineage, Mahendra Baahubali vows to avenge his father's death and reclaim the throne from the tyrannical Bhallaladeva. With the help of his loyal companion Kattappa and the support of his mother Devasena, Mahendra embarks on a perilous journey to overthrow the oppressive regime. Along the way, he confronts formidable enemies, uncovers shocking betrayals, and ultimately fulfills his destiny as the rightful ruler of Mahishmati.

Actors: Prabhas, Anushka Shetty, Rana Daggubati
Director: S.S. Rajamouli
