A skilled assassin, falsely accused of a politician's murder, takes refuge in the home of a kind-hearted woman. While posing as her long-lost cousin, he becomes entangled in a web of deception, discovering family secrets and confronting his own past. As the truth unfolds, he seeks justice against those who framed him. 

Actors: Mahesh Babu, Trisha
Director: Trivikram Srinivas
