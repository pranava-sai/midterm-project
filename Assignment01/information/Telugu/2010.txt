A man named Seetarama Raju, mistaken for a godman, becomes embroiled in a series of events involving a mysterious village plagued by drought and corruption. Alongside a taxi driver named Subhashini, he uncovers the truth behind the village's misfortunes and confronts the powerful forces at play. As the villagers rally behind him, Seetarama Raju emerges as their unlikely savior, bringing hope and justice to their lives.

Actors: Mahesh Babu, Anushka Shetty
Director: Trivikram Srinivas
