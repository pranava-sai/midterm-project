A rugged and resourceful smuggler named Pushpa, rises to prominence in the illegal sandalwood trade, facing off against ruthless adversaries and navigating treacherous terrain. His journey is marked by hardship, sacrifice, and a relentless pursuit of power and survival in the unforgiving forests of Andhra Pradesh. As tensions escalate and alliances shift, Pushpa's resilience and determination are put to the ultimate test.

Actors: Allu Arjun, Rashmika Mandanna
Director: Sukumar
