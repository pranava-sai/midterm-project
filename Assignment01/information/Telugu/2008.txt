A carefree young man, Sanjay Sahu, accidentally stumbles upon a political conspiracy involving a prominent leader. Falsely implicated in a murder, Sanjay goes on the run and seeks refuge in a small village. Assuming a new identity, he becomes part of a traditional family, only to find himself caught in a complex web of relationships and secrets. As he unravels the truth, he must confront his past and bring justice to those who framed him.

Actors: Pawan Kalyan, Ileana D'Cruz
Director: Trivikram Srinivas
