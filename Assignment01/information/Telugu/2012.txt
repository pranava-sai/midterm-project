A fearless and eccentric cop, Gabbar Singh, returns to his hometown and confronts corruption in the police force. Unconventional in his methods, he clashes with the local goon Siddhappa Naidu. Amidst the chaos, he falls for Bhagyalakshmi, the daughter of a local chief. The film explores Gabbar Singh's journey as he fights against injustice and strives to bring about positive change.

Actors: Pawan Kalyan, Shruti Haasan
Director: Harish Shankar
